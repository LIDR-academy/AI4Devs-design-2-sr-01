# 📊 Suggested Metrics and Data Privacy Measures for LTI

## ✅ 1. Suggested Metrics

### AI Accuracy

- **Precision & Recall** — Evaluate resume parsing, candidate ranking, or match
  scoring quality.
- **F1 Score** — Balance between precision & recall for candidate
  recommendation.
- **Error Rate** — Frequency of misclassification or incorrect auto-suggestions.
- **User Override Rate** — How often recruiters adjust AI-suggested results.
- **Time to Match** — Average time to generate top candidate matches.
- **Feedback Loop Accuracy** — Improvement in AI based on user corrections.

### User Satisfaction

- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** — Likelihood that users recommend LTI to others.
- **System Usability Scale (SUS)** — Standard measure of perceived ease of use.
- **Task Success Rate** — Percentage of tasks completed without needing support.
- **Time-on-Task** — Average time to complete core tasks.
- **User Feedback Trends** — Insights from surveys and feedback sessions.
- **Adoption Rate** — Usage rate of AI features compared to manual workflows.

---

## ✅ 2. Suggested Candidate Data Privacy Measures

- **Data Minimization:** Collect only essential candidate data.
- **Explicit Consent:** Obtain clear consent for data storage and processing.
- **Data Encryption:** Use strong encryption (e.g., AES-256, TLS) for data at
  rest and in transit.
- **Access Controls:** Enforce role-based permissions for viewing/modifying
  data.
- **Audit Logs:** Track and monitor data access and changes.
- **Data Retention Policy:** Define and enforce secure data deletion timelines.
- **Anonymization:** Use anonymized data sets for AI training where possible.
- **Compliance:** Follow GDPR, CCPA, or applicable privacy laws.
- **Breach Response Plan:** Implement procedures for detecting, reporting, and
  responding to breaches.
